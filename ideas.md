### Idea 1: Neo-Art Deco Opulence

**Design Movement:** Art Deco Revival meets Contemporary Luxury

**Core Principles:**
- Geometric precision with organic softness — sharp angles softened by warm gold tones

**Color Philosophy:**
- Deep Ivory (`#FAF6F0`) background — warm, not clinical
- Rich Champagne Gold (`#C9A96E`) as the primary accent — evokes luxury without ostentation

**Layout Paradigm:**
- Horizontal split hero: left text panel (60%) + right image panel (40%) with gold border frame
- Services in a diagonal-offset card grid
- Testimonials in overlapping quote cards
- Footer with geometric gold divider line

**Signature Elements:**
- Thin gold geometric border frames around images
- Art Deco fan/sunburst motif as a subtle background texture
- Serif display font with tracked uppercase subheadings

**Interaction Philosophy:**
- Gold underline slides in on nav hover
- Cards lift with a subtle gold shadow on hover
- Section entrances: fade-up with slight scale

**Animation:**
- Entrance: staggered fade-up (0.1s delay between elements)
- Hover: 200ms ease-out gold shadow bloom
- Scroll: parallax on hero image (subtle 20% offset)

**Typography System:**
- Display: `Playfair Display` — serif, elegant, editorial
- Body: `Lato` — clean, readable, modern contrast
- Accent labels: `Cormorant Garamond` italic

### Idea 2: Japanese Wabi-Sabi Minimalism

**Design Movement:** Wabi-Sabi meets Scandinavian Minimalism

**Core Principles:**
- Imperfect beauty — organic textures, natural materials, handcrafted feel
- Extreme whitespace as a luxury signal
- Monochromatic palette with one deliberate accent
- Content-first: typography IS the design

**Color Philosophy:**
- Warm White (`#F9F7F4`) — like handmade paper
- Warm Stone (`#8B7D6B`) for body text — softer than black
- Terracotta (`#C4714A`) as the single accent — earthy, warm, unexpected
- Sage Green (`#7A8C6E`) for subtle secondary elements

**Layout Paradigm:**
- Full-bleed editorial layout: text and images share equal weight
- Services as a vertical list with large numbers and thin dividers
- Gallery in an asymmetric masonry grid
- Generous line-height and letter-spacing throughout

**Signature Elements:**
- Brushstroke SVG dividers between sections
- Large, airy section numbers (01, 02, 03...)
- Grain/noise texture overlay on all backgrounds

**Interaction Philosophy:**
- Minimal hover states — subtle opacity shift
- No aggressive animations — content reveals gently
- Cursor changes to a custom dot on interactive elements

**Animation:**
- Entrance: slow fade-in (0.6s ease) — no movement, just opacity
- Hover: 300ms opacity transition
- Scroll: no parallax — stillness is intentional

**Typography System:**
- Display: `Cormorant Garamond` — high contrast, literary
- Body: `Source Serif 4` — warm, readable
- Labels: `Space Mono` — technical contrast

## Selected Approach: **Idea 1 — Neo-Art Deco Opulence** (Adapted for PhiBrows)

**Brand:** PhiBrows — Premium Microblading, Eyelashes, Laser Pigmentation Removal & Permanent Lipstick

**Adapted Color Palette:**
- Deep Black (`#0A0A0A`) — sophisticated, premium
- Crisp White (`#FFFFFF`) — clean, medical-grade luxury
- Soft Gray (`#F5F5F5`) — warm off-white for breathing room
- Rose Gold Accent (`#D4A5A5`) — soft feminine touch, luxury warmth

The black-and-white foundation communicates precision and professionalism (essential for beauty procedures), while rose gold accents add warmth and femininity. This creates a high-end, medical-spa aesthetic that feels both luxurious and trustworthy.

**Design Rationale:** Art Deco geometry with black/white sophistication perfectly suits a precision-based beauty service. The geometric frames and serif typography convey expertise and elegance, while the soft rose gold accents prevent coldness and maintain the feminine, approachable brand voice.
